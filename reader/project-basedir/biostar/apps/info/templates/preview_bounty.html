Bounty is a sum of money to incentivise a good answer

  1. Bounty is anonymous
  2. Anyone can increase the current bounty
  3. Bounty is awarded to the answer with the most votes
  4. Answers that existed before the Bounty was started are excluded from the bounty process
  5. Bounty is awarded 3 days after the first answer is posted unless there is a better answer
  6. Every time a better answer is posted the 3 day timer resets
  7. If the answer author does not take custody of the bounty sats within 7 days the award becomes available to the next best answer
